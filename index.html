Satellite IoT Systems & Services

The first Egyptian company that describes itself as an “Space-as-a-service start-up” founded in 2022, has been working on its “antennas”, and interested with satellite IoT-related products and services that it says will be affordable enough for a broader variety of businesses to track and monitor every sensor. Egypt Space Systems is being targeted at a wide variety of satellite IoT products, services and data analytics applications, in sectors such as oil and gas, forestry, mining, and military. It also is being aimed in particular at areas where cellular and other connectivity options may not be available.
